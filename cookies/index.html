Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to site owners. Cookies do not contain personal information by themselves, but they may be linked to personal data you provide through forms or other interactions.

2. How We Use Cookies

The Studio X Wrestling website uses a minimal set of cookies to improve your experience:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as remembering your cookie consent preference.

  • sx-cookie-consent — Stores your cookie consent preference (accepted or declined) so we don't show the banner on every visit. Expires when you clear browser storage.

Analytics Cookies (if enabled)

We may use basic analytics to understand how visitors use our site (e.g., which pages are most popular, how long visitors stay). If we use a third-party analytics service, it may set its own cookies. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our site includes links and embeds from third-party services. These services may set their own cookies when you interact with them:

  • Google Maps — When you click our address link to Google Maps, Google may set cookies on their domain. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Instagram — Clicking Instagram links or viewing embedded content may result in Instagram cookies being set. See Instagram's Privacy Policy.

We have no control over third-party cookies. Please review those services' cookie policies directly.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies currently on your device and set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, doing so may require you to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site.

Browser-specific instructions:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

5. Your Consent

When you first visit our website, we display a cookie consent banner. By clicking "Accept," you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or clicking "Decline" if the banner reappears after clearing.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
